Toddlers

Our toddlers’ very busy day activities include art, reading, music, second language, sign language, and outdoor play. Designed with the curious child in mind, the toddler daycare at our early education center comprises monthly themes reinforced with books, puppets, songs, free-form art projects, and other fun activities to nurture their creativity.

Curriculum elements include(Creative Curriculum Website):

  • Color Recognition
  • Language Development
  • Phonological Awareness: Songs, Rhymes, and Finger Plays
  • Self-Concept
  • Small and Large Muscle Development
  • Social-Emotional Development

At our daycare center, we love working with toddlers…and it shows! We know how formative these early years are, so our early childhood education programs are designed to stimulate the little ones’ brains as much as possible in a way that speaks to them. If you have questions or want to schedule an appointment with our daycare and preschool center, contact us today!
